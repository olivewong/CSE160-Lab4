Bouncy Phong Light

You can click the checkboxes to disable normal/lighting and use the sliders to control angle and light position. I tried to optimize by having separate render and initialize shape functions to minimize the amount of re-assigning variables and making new buffers. It has specular, diffuse, and ambient. Note: the show normal works best without lighting. Behind the blocks is my blocky, legless loaf.
